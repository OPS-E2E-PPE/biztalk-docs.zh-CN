 <span data-ttu-id="4166f-137">可以解决此难题就各种不同的名称： 业务流程自动化 (BPA)、 业务流程管理 (BPM) 和其他人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4166f-137">Addressing this challenge goes by various names: business process automation (BPA), business process management (BPM), and others.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4166f-138">不管名称如何，这两个方案对于应用程序集成都至关重要。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4166f-138">Regardless of the name, two scenarios are most important for application integration.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4166f-139">一个方案用于连接单个组织内的应用程序，通常称为企业应用程序集成 (EAI)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4166f-139">One is connecting applications within a single organization, commonly referred to as enterprise application integration (EAI).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4166f-140">另一个方案称为企业对企业 (B2B) 集成，用于连接不同组织中的应用程序。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4166f-140">The other, called business-to-business (B2B) integration, connects applications in different organizations.</span></span>
